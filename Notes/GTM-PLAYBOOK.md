## ðŸ› ï¸ Channel Tactics

### Twitter (Primary)
**Frequency:** 2-3 posts/day  
**Mix:**
- 40% Educational (AI trends, ops tips)
- 30% Product updates (features, use cases)
- 20% Personal founder journey
- 10% Engagement (replies, memes)

**Content Types:**
- Threads (3-10 tweets) on AI/ops topics
- Screenshots of employee outputs
- Customer wins (with permission)
- Hot takes on AI industry

**Growth Tactics:**
- Reply to big accounts (add value, not spam)
- Quote tweet with insights
- Use relevant hashtags (#AI #Startup #Operations)
- Collaborate with other AI founders

### LinkedIn (Secondary)
**Frequency:** 1 post/day  
**Mix:**
- 50% Professional insights (long-form)
- 30% Product/company updates
- 20% Personal/culture

**Content Types:**
- "How I built..." stories
- Industry trend analysis
- Employee spotlights (even AI employees)
- Behind-the-scenes

### Product Hunt (Launch)
**Pre-Launch:**
- Build email list of supporters
- Create teaser trailer
- Warm up PH community (comment, engage)

**Launch Day:**
- Post at 12:01 AM PST
- Reply to every comment within 5 minutes
- Share across all channels
- Ask network to upvote (don't beg)

**Post-Launch:**
- Thank supporters publicly
- Analyze what worked
- Convert hunters to customers

### Email/Newsletter
**Frequency:** Weekly  
**Format:**
- Subject line: Curiosity-driven
- Opening: Personal update (2 sentences)
- Body: 1 big idea + 3 supporting points
- CTA: Soft (demo, reply, share)

**Content:**
- AI industry insights
- Customer stories
- Product deep-dives
- Founder lessons
