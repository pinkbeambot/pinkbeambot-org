### 1. Adept AI (adept.ai)
**What they do:** General-purpose AI agent that uses a browser
**Funding:** $415M (Series B)
**Positioning:** "Use AI to do anything you can do on a computer"

**Strengths:**

- General-purpose (any workflow)

**Weaknesses:**

- Faster time-to-value (15 min setup vs. weeks)

### 2. Relevance AI (relevanceai.com)
**What they do:** Build AI agents for sales/marketing teams
**Funding:** $10M+ (Series A)
**Positioning:** "Build your AI workforce"

**Strengths:**
- Closer to our positioning
- Good integrations (Salesforce, HubSpot)
- Strong in sales use case

**Weaknesses:**
- DIY platform (customers build their own agents)
- Requires technical setup
- Narrow focus (only sales/marketing)

**How we win:**
- Pre-built employees (not DIY)
- Broader use cases (research, support, content)
- Better UX for non-technical users

**Differentiation:** *"Relevance makes you build employees. We hire them for you."*

### 3. AutoGPT / BabyAGI (Open Source)
**What they do:** Autonomous AI agents that self-direct

**Differentiation:** *"AutoGPT is a research project. We're production infrastructure."*

## ğŸ›¡ï¸ Moat Analysis

### What Protects Us

1. **Integration Depth**
   - Not just API connections, but "employee training"
   - Deep context about customer's business
   - Hard to replicate quickly

2. **Cross-Functional Coordination**
   - The handoffs between employees are complex
   - Requires understanding of business workflows
   - Not just technical integration

3. **Vertical Expertise**
   - We understand what a "good researcher" or "good SDR" does
   - Built into the product
   - Hard for generalists to match

4. **Customer Context**
   - Employees learn customer's specific business over time
   - Switching cost increases with tenure
   - Network effects (employees share learnings)

### What Doesn't Protect Us

1. **Technology**
   - Using OpenAI/Anthropic APIs (commoditized)
   - OpenClaw (could be replicated)
   - Need to build proprietary layers

2. **First Mover**
   - Adept and others are ahead
   - Need to out-execute, not just be first
