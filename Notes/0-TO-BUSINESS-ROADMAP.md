## üö¶ Decision Gates

**Gate 1: End of Phase 0**
- Criteria: Validated problem, working prototype, confident team
- If NO: Pivot or kill

**Gate 2: End of Phase 1**
- Criteria: 10 paying customers, <10% churn, clear PMF signals
- If NO: Narrow focus, extend phase, or pivot

**Gate 3: End of Phase 2**
- Criteria: 50 customers, $75K MRR, strong unit economics
- If NO: Fix unit economics or focus on profitable segment

**Gate 4: End of Phase 3**
- Criteria: $4M+ ARR, category leadership, clear path to $10M+
- If NO: Evaluate acquisition, double down, or niche down

## üí∞ Funding Strategy

**Phase 0-1:** Self-funded / Revenue-funded
- Target: No external funding needed until $50K MRR
- Advantage: Maintain control, no dilution

**Phase 2:** Optional seed round
- Target: $1-2M at $8-10M valuation
- Use of funds: Team expansion, enterprise features
- Only raise if growth outpaces cash flow

**Phase 3:** Series A
- Target: $5-10M at $30-50M valuation
- Use of funds: Scale sales, international, platform
- Timing: When hitting $300K+ MRR with strong growth
