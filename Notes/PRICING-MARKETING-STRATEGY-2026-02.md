## Part 1: Pricing Model Analysis

### 1.1 Current Tier Structure Review

| Tier | Monthly | Annual | Target | Assessment |
|------|---------|--------|--------|------------|
| **Signal** | $29 | $278 | Side hustlers | ‚úÖ Well-positioned for entry market |
| **Satellite** | $79 | $758 | Small business | ‚úÖ Strong value at this price point |
| **Pink Beam** | $299 | $2,870 | Growing business | ‚úÖ Sweet spot for serious buyers |
| **Full VALIS** | $999 | $9,590 | Established business | ‚úÖ Premium positioning works |
| **Enterprise** | $2,499+ | Custom | Large orgs | ‚úÖ Leaves room for negotiation |

**Verdict:** The pricing ladder is well-designed. The gaps between tiers create clear upgrade paths without overlap.

### 1.2 Competitor Benchmarking

Based on market knowledge of AI website/automation agencies:

| Competitor Type | Price Range | Our Position |
|-----------------|-------------|--------------|
| **DIY AI Builders** (Wix AI, Framer) | $20-50/mo | We compete on value, not price |
| **AI Chatbot Services** (Intercom, Drift) | $75-500/mo | Comparable mid-tier pricing |
| **Full-Service AI Agencies** | $2,000-10K/mo | Our Enterprise tier is competitive |
| **Traditional Web Dev** | $5K-50K upfront | We win on speed + ongoing value |

**Positioning:** We sit between DIY tools and full-service agencies ‚Äî "agency quality at SaaS prices."

### 1.3 Cost Analysis (Preliminary)

| Component | Est. Monthly Cost/Customer | Notes |
|-----------|---------------------------|-------|
| **OpenAI API** | $5-50 | Varies by tier/interactions |
| **Cloudflare Workers** | $2-10 | Based on request volume |
| **D1/KV Storage** | $1-5 | Data persistence |
| **Bandwidth** | $1-3 | Image/assets delivery |
| **Infrastructure Total** | $9-68 | Low at scale |

**Margin Analysis:**
- Signal ($29): Thin margin (~60% gross) but customer acquisition play
- Pink Beam ($299): Healthy margin (~75% gross) ‚Äî profit driver
- Full VALIS ($999): Excellent margin (~85% gross) ‚Äî whale hunting

### 1.4 Setup Fee Recommendation

**Current:** No setup fee (pure SaaS model)

**Recommendation:** Add tiered setup fees

| Tier | Setup Fee | Justification |
|------|-----------|---------------|
| Signal | $199 | Covers onboarding time |
| Satellite | $299 | Higher touch implementation |
| Pink Beam | $399 | Custom configuration |
| Full VALIS | $499 | White-glove setup |
| Enterprise | Custom | Included in negotiation |

**Rationale:**
- Increases immediate revenue and CAC payback
- Signals "real service" vs. just software
- Customers who pay setup fees are more committed (better retention)
- Industry standard for agency-like services
