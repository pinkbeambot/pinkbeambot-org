- Just lost a key employee (realized they're vulnerable)
- Just missed a major competitor move (realized they're blind)
- Just spent a weekend catching up on email (ready to automate)

- **"What if it makes a mistake?"**
  - Counter: "What if your human employee makes a mistake? We build in oversight."
  
- **"I don't want to lose the human touch"**
  - Counter: "This frees your humans to be more human. Less grunt work."
  
- **"$2,000/month is expensive"**
  - Counter: "That's 1/3 the cost of one employee, and you get 3 of them."
