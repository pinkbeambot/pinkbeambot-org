# Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

## General Questions

### What exactly is Pink Beam Bot?
Pink Beam Bot is an AI-powered platform that creates intelligent agents for your website. Using our VALIS system, you can deploy chatbots that answer customer questions, reference your specific business information, and operate 24/7 without human intervention.

### How is this different from ChatGPT or other AI chatbots?
Unlike generic AI chatbots, Pink Beam Bot:
- Uses YOUR specific business knowledge and documents
- Provides cited, accurate information from your knowledge base
- Operates autonomously within your defined parameters
- Integrates with your existing systems and workflows
- Maintains conversation context across sessions

### Do I need technical knowledge to use Pink Beam Bot?
Not at all! Our platform is designed for business owners, not developers. You can:
- Upload documents through a simple web interface
- Configure your AI agent with point-and-click settings
- Deploy the chat widget with a simple copy-paste code snippet
- Monitor performance through an intuitive dashboard

## Technical Questions

### What websites does it work with?
Pink Beam Bot works with any website that can embed JavaScript:
- WordPress
- Shopify
- Wix
- Squarespace
- Custom HTML sites
- React/Next.js applications
- And any other platform!

### Will it slow down my website?
No. Our chat widget loads asynchronously and has minimal impact on page load times. It's optimized for performance with:
- Lazy loading (only loads when needed)
- Compressed assets
- CDN distribution
- Caching for instant responses

### Is my data secure?
Yes! We take security seriously:
- All data encrypted in transit (TLS/SSL)
- Databases encrypted at rest
- Multi-tenant isolation (your data is never mixed with others)
- SOC 2 Type II compliant infrastructure
- Regular security audits
- You own your data and can export/delete anytime

### Where is my data stored?
Data is stored in secure, redundant data centers:
- Primary: US East (Virginia)
- Backup: US West (California)
- Enterprise customers can request EU or other regions

### Do you offer on-premise deployment?
Yes! Enterprise customers can opt for on-premise deployment for maximum control and security.
