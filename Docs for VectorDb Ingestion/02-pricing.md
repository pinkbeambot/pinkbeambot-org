## Frequently Asked Pricing Questions

### Can I upgrade or downgrade at any time?
Yes! You can change your plan at any time. Upgrades take effect immediately. Downgrades take effect at the end of your billing cycle.

### Is there a free trial?
Yes! All plans include a 14-day free trial. No credit card required to start.

### What happens if I exceed my query limit?
Additional queries are charged at $0.01 per query. We'll notify you when you reach 80% of your limit.

### Do you offer annual billing discounts?
Yes! Save 20% when you pay annually. Contact us for enterprise annual contracts.

### Can I add individual engines to my plan?
Absolutely! You can purchase individual engine add-ons for $49/month each.

### Is there a setup fee?
No setup fees for Satellite, Pink Beam, or Full VALIS tiers. Enterprise implementations may have custom setup costs based on complexity.

### What payment methods do you accept?
We accept all major credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express), PayPal, and bank transfers for annual plans.

### Do you offer refunds?
We offer a 30-day money-back guarantee for all plans. If you're not satisfied, contact us for a full refund.
